Hard Rule
No 0DTE entries during the first 20 minutes. The Opening Range (ORB) is being established. Any trade before 9:50 AM is pure gambling — let the market show its hand.

The Only Rule for Entry Direction
Price direction at ORB breakout = your trade direction. Simple. If price breaks ORB High → calls. If it breaks ORB Low → puts. Don't fight it. Let the market decide.

Step 5.2
Watch for GEX Flip notification

If you set up ntfy alerts, you'll receive a push notification when price crosses the GEX flip zone. This is the single most important mid-trade signal:

GEX Flip Alert = Regime Change
When price crosses the ⬡ flip zone, market behavior switches. If you're in a trending trade (NEG GEX), the move may stall. If in a range trade (POS GEX), a new trend could begin. Treat the flip zone as your stop or at minimum a position-size reduction trigger.
Step 5.3
Time-of-day awareness
9:50 AM – 11:00 AM ET
Prime window

Maximum theta + directional momentum. Best entries and highest R:R. Most 0DTE profits made here.

11:00 AM – 1:00 PM ET
Chop zone

Institutional lunch hour. Low conviction. Avoid new entries unless a catalyst breaks the range. Manage existing positions.

1:00 PM – 2:30 PM ET
Secondary window

Second best window. Bond market closes at 3 PM driving equity flows. Watch for resumption of morning trend.

2:30 PM – 3:45 PM ET
Theta crush zone — danger

0DTE contracts lose 50–70% of remaining value in the last 90 minutes unless deep ITM. Hold only if highly confident. Most losses happen from holding too long.

3:45 PM – 4:00 PM ET
Close — no new entries

Gamma explosion territory. OTM contracts go to zero. Any remaining 0DTE position that isn't deep ITM should be closed.

Phase 6
Exit Protocol
Phase 6
Exits — Pre-planned, not reactive
While in a position
Step 6.1
Take profit at Call/Put Walls

GEX walls are the highest concentration of dealer hedging. Price often stalls, reverses, or consolidates at these levels. They are targets, not ceilings.

  • At Target 1 (first wall): Close 50–70% of position
  • Move stop to breakeven on remainder
  • At Target 2 (next wall or time-based): Close remainder
Step 6.2
Stop-loss triggers

Exit the entire position immediately if:

  • Price closes a 5-min candle back inside the ORB (false breakout)
  • Price crosses the GEX Flip Zone in the wrong direction
  • The Fire Card flips to the opposite regime
  • Time is past 2:30 PM ET and position is not deep ITM
  • Option loses 50% of entry premium (hard stop on premium)
Non-Negotiable Stop
Pre-set your stop in the broker before entering the trade. A stop set in advance doesn't require a decision under pressure. A mental stop is not a stop.
Step 6.3
End of day — log your trade

After closing, return to Ch0plife and log the trade in your journal. Note:

  • Symbol, direction (call/put), strike, expiry
  • Entry price, exit price, P&L
  • GEX regime at entry, GOOD BONES score
  • What worked / what you would change
Why this matters
The scanner backtest system logs every signal with a score. Reviewing your own trades against the scanner's signal history reveals whether you're trading the edge — or trading around it.
